Services

Our Services

Endocrinology

Our doctor specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of hormonal disorders. These services may include issues related to the endocrine system such as thyroid, adrenal glands, diabetes, and obesity.

Diabetes Services

Diabetes management and treatment include blood sugar control, insulin therapy, nutrition counseling, and prevention of complications. Personalized treatment plans are provided to diabetes patients.

Thyroid Services

These services focus on the health of the thyroid gland, including monitoring thyroid hormone levels, diagnosing thyroid disorders such as nodules or goiter, and providing appropriate treatment methods.

Dietary Services

Our clinic offers dietary counseling services for diabetes patients. Healthy eating is crucial in diabetes management. Our dietitians create individualized diet plans, support blood sugar control, and promote a healthy lifestyle.
